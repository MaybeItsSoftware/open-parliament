Open Parliament lets you read UK Parliamentary debates verbatim from Hansard.

Features:
- Read debates from the House of Commons and House of Lords.
- Full offline support: search and read cached debates without an internet connection.
- Track bills, member profiles, and voting records.
- View live parliament broadcast schedules.

Data sources:
This app displays public data retrieved live from the official UK Parliament APIs:
- Hansard (debate transcripts): https://hansard-api.parliament.uk
- Members API (MP and Lords profiles, voting records): https://members-api.parliament.uk
- Bills API (bill stages and news): https://bills-api.parliament.uk
- Parliament Live (broadcast schedules): https://parliamentlive.tv
